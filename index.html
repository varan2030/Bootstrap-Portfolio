About Me

My name is Almaz. Almaz means a diamond. I am from Kyrgyzstan, a country in Central Asia. Graduated in 2003 from The International University of Kyrgyzstan I started working in National Bank (Central Bank) of Kyrgyzstan for 6 years. In 2009 I moved to Almaty, Kazakhstan. I worked there in a bank as an Internal auditor. In 2010 I realized that I wanted to run my own business. I set up a farm in the homeland. I was really excited to grow livestock and produce dairy products. Last winter I relocated to DC, but I still have the farm in my country. I like traveling over the world. I have been to France, England, Germany, Indonesia, Malaysia, Dominicana Republic, Turkey, Uzbekistan, Armenia and Russia. I would like to visit as many countries as possible.
